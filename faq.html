Frequently Asked Questions

Searcher for Discogs is the Google Chrome browser extension for the Discogs site. With it you can discover new music and searching for records to buy more easily. Just click on any track in any release and you will see a popup with a video.

Extension supports YouTube, Spotify and Deezer. It uses YouTube by default. You can change default video source on settings page.

Unfortunately not all tracks are available in the internet. If it is not on YouTube, try Spotify or Deezer.

It has 7 days trial. Then you can subscribe and be charged on a recurring basis every month. Price varies by country. You will see the price for your country in the Chrome Web Store. For example for USA it is 0.99$.

To be able to access and handle video sources and license information.

Unfortunately, we do not manage payments. You should ask Google support or call your bank.

You can unsubscribe at any time and then, if you wish, subscribe again. You can manage your subscriptions here.
